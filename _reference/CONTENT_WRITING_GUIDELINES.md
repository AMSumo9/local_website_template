## Why This Matters

AI-generated content has telltale patterns that make it:
- Less trustworthy to readers
- Generic and impersonal
- Potentially penalized by search engines
- Harder to connect with emotionally

**Goal:** Create content that sounds like it was written by a real person who cares about helping the reader.

---

## Common AI Writing Hallmarks

### 1. Em Dashes (—)

**❌ AI Hallmark:**
```
Our service offers comprehensive solutions—from initial consultation to final settlement—ensuring a smooth experience.
```

**✓ Natural Alternative:**
```
Our service offers comprehensive solutions. From initial consultation to final settlement, we ensure a smooth experience.
```

Or use commas:
```
Our service offers comprehensive solutions, from initial consultation to final settlement, ensuring a smooth experience.
```

**Rule:** Replace em dashes (—) with periods, commas, or parentheses.

---

### 2. Overused Transition Phrases

**❌ AI Hallmarks:**
- "It's worth noting that..."
- "It's important to note that..."
- "It should be noted that..."
- "In conclusion..."
- "In summary..."
- "Moving forward..."
- "Let's delve into..."
- "Let's explore..."
- "Let's dive into..."
- "At the end of the day..."
- "When it comes to..."

**✓ Natural Alternatives:**
- Just state the fact directly
- "Remember that..."
- "Keep in mind..."
- Simply start with the point
- Use section headings instead
- Be direct and specific

**Examples:**

❌ "It's worth noting that our interest rates are competitive."
✓ "Our interest rates are competitive."

❌ "When it comes to home loans, we offer flexible options."
✓ "We offer flexible home loan options."

---

### 3. Overly Formal or Verbose Language

**❌ AI Hallmarks:**
- "Leverage our expertise"
- "Utilize our services"
- "Facilitate your needs"
- "Endeavour to provide"
- "Ascertain your requirements"
- "In order to"
- "For the purpose of"
- "With regard to"
- "In the event that"

**✓ Natural Alternatives:**
- "Use our expertise"
- "Use our services"
- "Meet your needs"
- "We work to provide" or "We aim to provide"
- "Find out what you need"
- "To"
- "To" or "For"
- "About"
- "If"

---

### 4. Repetitive Sentence Structures

**❌ AI Pattern:**
```
Our team provides expert advice. Our brokers offer personalized service. Our company ensures fast approvals.
```

**✓ Natural Variation:**
```
Our team provides expert advice. You'll get personalized service from experienced brokers, and we make sure approvals happen quickly.
```

**Rule:** Vary sentence length, structure, and starting words.

---

### 5. Unnecessary Metaphors and Flowery Language

**❌ AI Hallmarks:**
- "Navigate the complex landscape of..."
- "Journey through the process..."
- "Unlock the potential of..."
- "Embark on your path to..."
- "Illuminate the way forward..."
- "Pave the road to success..."

**✓ Natural Alternatives:**
- "Understand..."
- "Go through the process..."
- "Make the most of..."
- "Start your path to..." or "Begin..."
- "Show you how..."
- "Help you succeed..."

**Examples:**

❌ "Navigate the complex landscape of home finance."
✓ "Understand home finance options."

❌ "Embark on your property ownership journey."
✓ "Start buying your property." or "Begin your property search."

---

### 6. Generic Superlatives Without Proof

**❌ AI Hallmarks:**
- "World-class service"
- "Unparalleled expertise"
- "Cutting-edge solutions"
- "Revolutionary approach"
- "Unprecedented results"

**✓ Natural Alternatives with Proof:**
- "5-star rated service (read our reviews)"
- "15+ years of experience in Brisbane"
- "Modern online tools"
- "Personalized approach"
- "Fast approvals (average 48 hours)"

**Rule:** If you make a claim, back it up with specifics.

---

### 7. Passive Voice Overuse

**❌ AI Pattern:**
```
Your application will be reviewed by our team. A decision will be made within 48 hours. You will be contacted with the results.
```

**✓ Active Voice:**
```
Our team reviews your application. We make a decision within 48 hours and contact you with the results.
```

**Rule:** Use active voice for 80%+ of sentences.

---

### 8. Lists with Semicolons

**❌ AI Format:**
```
We offer: competitive rates; fast approvals; expert guidance; local knowledge.
```

**✓ Natural Format:**
```
We offer:
- Competitive rates
- Fast approvals
- Expert guidance
- Local knowledge
```

Or: "We offer competitive rates, fast approvals, expert guidance, and local knowledge."

## Formatting Issues

### Remove These AI Indicators

1. **Em Dashes (—)**: Replace with hyphens (-), commas, or periods
2. **Excessive Semicolons**: Use periods or bullet points instead
3. **Elaborate Headings**: Keep headings simple and clear
4. **Over-formatted Text**: Don't overuse bold, italics, or capitalization

### Example Cleanup

**❌ Before:**
```
# Comprehensive Financial Solutions—Your Path to Success

In today's complex financial landscape—navigating the myriad options available—can be overwhelming; however, our team is here to help.
```

**✓ After:**
```
# Financial Solutions to Help You Succeed

Finding the right financial options can be overwhelming. Our team is here to help.
```

## Language and Style

### Write Like You Talk

**Test:** Read your content aloud. Does it sound like something you'd say to a friend?

**❌ Sounds like AI:**
```
It is important to note that, when seeking financial assistance, one must carefully consider all available options in order to ascertain the most suitable solution for one's unique circumstances.
```

**✓ Sounds human:**
```
When you're looking for financial help, take time to compare your options. This helps you find the best fit for your situation.
```

---

### Use Contractions

**❌ Too formal:**
```
We will help you find a solution. You will not be disappointed.
```

**✓ Natural:**
```
We'll help you find a solution. You won't be disappointed.
```

**Contractions to use:**
- we'll, you'll, they'll
- we're, you're, they're
- don't, won't, can't, isn't, aren't
- it's, that's, what's

---

### Be Specific, Not Generic

**❌ Generic AI:**
```
We provide excellent service to our valued clients in the local area.
```

**✓ Specific and Real:**
```
We've helped over 500 families in Brisbane buy their first homes since 2015.
```

---

### Show Personality

**❌ Robotic:**
```
Our organization provides financial services to individuals seeking home loan solutions.
```

**✓ Human:**
```
We help people like you get home loans. It's what we love doing.
```

## How to Write Naturally

### 1. Start with Why

Don't lead with features. Lead with benefits.

**❌ Feature-first:**
```
Our platform utilizes advanced algorithms to match you with suitable lenders.
```

**✓ Benefit-first:**
```
We find you the best lenders for your situation—quickly and easily.
```

---

### 2. Use Second Person (You/Your)

**❌ Third person:**
```
Clients can expect personalized service tailored to their needs.
```

**✓ Second person:**
```
You'll get personalized service tailored to your needs.
```

---

### 3. Keep Sentences Short

Mix short and long sentences, but avoid consistently long ones.

**❌ Too long:**
```
Our comprehensive service offering, which includes initial consultation, documentation preparation, lender comparison, application submission, and ongoing support, ensures that clients receive end-to-end assistance throughout their entire home loan journey.
```

**✓ Better:**
```
We help you through every step of getting a home loan. This includes your initial consultation, preparing documents, comparing lenders, submitting applications, and ongoing support.
```

---

### 4. Use Simple Words

| Instead of... | Use... |
|---|---|
| Utilize | Use |
| Endeavor | Try |
| Facilitate | Help |
| Ascertain | Find out |
| Subsequently | Then, Next |
| Prior to | Before |
| In order to | To |
| With regard to | About |

---

### 5. Cut Unnecessary Words

**❌ Wordy:**
```
In the event that you find yourself in need of assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to our team.
```

**✓ Concise:**
```
If you need help, contact our team.
```

## Editing Checklist

### Step 1: Search and Replace

Search your content for these AI hallmarks and replace them:

- [ ] Em dashes (—) → Replace with comma, period, or hyphen (-)
- [ ] "It's worth noting" → Delete or replace with direct statement
- [ ] "Let's delve into" → Replace with "Let's look at" or just start the topic
- [ ] "Navigate the landscape" → Replace with simpler phrasing
- [ ] "Leverage" → Replace with "use"
- [ ] "Utilize" → Replace with "use"
- [ ] "In order to" → Replace with "to"
- [ ] Semicolons in lists → Replace with bullet points

### Step 2: Read Aloud

- [ ] Does it sound natural when spoken?
- [ ] Are there tongue-twisters or overly complex phrases?
- [ ] Does it sound like something a real person would say?

### Step 3: Check Patterns

- [ ] Do most sentences start differently?
- [ ] Is there variety in sentence length?
- [ ] Have you used contractions where natural?
- [ ] Is the tone conversational, not academic?

### Step 4: Verify Specificity

- [ ] Are claims backed by specifics (numbers, examples)?
- [ ] Have you removed generic superlatives?
- [ ] Is local relevance clear?

### Step 5: Remove Fluff

- [ ] Have you cut unnecessary transition phrases?
- [ ] Is every word earning its place?
- [ ] Could you say it simpler?

## Before and After Examples

### Example 1: Service Page

**❌ AI-Generated Style:**
```
It's worth noting that when it comes to navigating the complex landscape of home finance, our comprehensive suite of services—ranging from initial consultation to final settlement—leverages cutting-edge technology to facilitate an unparalleled experience. We endeavour to provide world-class guidance; ensuring that clients can successfully embark on their property ownership journey.
```

**✓ Natural Human Style:**
```
Getting a home loan doesn't have to be complicated. We guide you through every step, from your first consultation to final settlement. With modern online tools and expert advice, we make the process smooth and simple. We've helped over 500 Brisbane families buy homes since 2015.
```

---

### Example 2: About Page

**❌ AI-Generated Style:**
```
Our organization, established with the purpose of revolutionizing the mortgage broking industry, seeks to deliver exceptional outcomes through our innovative approach. It is important to note that our team of highly qualified professionals possesses extensive expertise in the field—enabling us to provide tailored solutions that address each client's unique circumstances.
```

**✓ Natural Human Style:**
```
We started this business to make mortgage broking better. Our team brings 50+ years of combined experience, and we use it to find the right loan for your situation. No jargon, no pressure—just honest advice.
```

---

### Example 3: Location Page

**❌ AI-Generated Style:**
```
Situated in the heart of Brisbane—a vibrant metropolis characterized by its thriving property market—our team of dedicated professionals endeavours to illuminate the path forward for prospective homeowners. Let's delve into the unique opportunities that Brisbane presents; opportunities that we are uniquely positioned to help you leverage.
```

**✓ Natural Human Style:**
```
Brisbane's property market is active and competitive. As local brokers, we know the area well. We help you find opportunities and secure the best rates. Let's talk about what's available in your target suburbs.
```

---

## Final Tips

1. **Write first, edit second**: Don't over-think the first draft. Write naturally, then edit.

2. **Get a second opinion**: Have someone else read it. Does it sound like you?

3. **Use real examples**: Include actual client situations (anonymized) where possible.

4. **Be yourself**: Your brand should have a voice. Let it come through.

5. **Less is more**: If you can say it in fewer words, do.

---

## Pre-Launch Content Review

Before launching, review ALL content for:

- [ ] No em dashes (—)
- [ ] No "It's worth noting" or similar phrases
- [ ] No overly formal language
- [ ] No unnecessary metaphors
- [ ] Active voice used predominantly
- [ ] Contractions used naturally
- [ ] Specific examples and numbers included
- [ ] Sentence variety (short and long mixed)
- [ ] Sounds natural when read aloud
- [ ] Personality evident in writing
- [ ] No generic superlatives without proof
- [ ] Local relevance clear
- [ ] Conversational tone throughout

---

## Remember

**Real people connect with real writing.** Your website should sound like you're having a conversation with a customer, not presenting an academic paper.

When in doubt, ask: **"Would I say this to someone in person?"**

If the answer is no, rewrite it.
